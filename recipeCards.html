Chocolate Strawberry Cake Rolls

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cups of flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/3 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/3 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 salt
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 cup of strawberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. For the cake roll, beat the eggs and sugar until light and fluffy in a mixing bowl.
  3. Pour in the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt into the egg mixture slowly.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Let it cool completely.
  6. For the filling, whip the heavy cream, powder sugar, and vanilla extract until a stiff peak forms.
  7. Add the strawberries into the filling batter and mix.
  8. Spread the filling and roll the cake.

Chocolate Raspberry Mousse Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of boiling water
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup raspberry jam
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 8 ounces dark chocolate, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Mix sugar, flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Mix and add eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract into the batter.
  4. Bake for 30-35 minutes. Let the cake cool for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, whip the heavy cream and sugar until a peak form.
  6. Beat the cream cheese until smooth and add the raspberry jam. Mix them both together.
  7. To make the ganache, heat the heavy cream and pour the dark chocolate. Let the ganache sit for 5 minutes.
  8. Spread the chocolate ganache and add some fresh raspberries on top of the cake.

S'mores Donuts

Ingredients

  • 3 cup of flour
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 2 1/4 teaspoon of active yeast
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 salt
  • Graham crackers
  • Chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, salt, water, and yeast. Let it sit for a while till it is foamy.
  2. Add the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract, and mix till the dough is formed.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface until smooth and elastic. place it in a greased bowl.
  4. Punch the dough and roll it up to a doughnut shape. Fry until it is golden brown.
  5. Once it is cooled, spread a layer of marshmallow creme on each doughnut
  6. Top it with graham cracker crumbs and chocolate chips.

Salted Caramel Chocolate Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1/3 Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 salt
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup of hot coffee
  • 3 ounces bittersweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1 cup salted caramel sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Whisk together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Mix the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat the eggs one at a time, then add the vanilla.
  4. Combine dry ingredients and hot coffee with the wet ingredients and mix well.
  5. Add some chocolate to the batter.
  6. Divide the chocolate batter evenly amount the cupcake tray, about 2/3 full.
  7. Bake for 18-22 minutes.
  8. Frost with salted caramel sauce.

Chocolate Cream & Cherries Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 mascarpone
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 3/4 heavy Cream
  • 1 1/4 sugar
  • 1 cup of coffee
  • 2 packs of ladyfingers
  • 1 teaspoon cocoa powder
  • 2 spoons of cherries preserve
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk the mascarpone and sugar until it is smooth.
  2. Whip the heavy cream until it forms a peak.
  3. Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture.
  4. Layer the ladyfingers with the mascarpone cream, cherry preserves, cocoa powder, and chocolate.
  5. Repeat the layers, finishing with a final layer of cocoa powder and chocolate.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 8 hours
  7. Slice and serve.

Raspberry Macarons

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup of almond flour
  • 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 3 egg whites
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tarter
  • 1/3 cup of sugar
  • 1 drop of red/pink food coloring
  • A pinch of cream of tartar
  • 1/4 cup of raspberry jam for filling
  • 6 ounces of fresh raspberries
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Sift the almond flour and powdered sugar in a bowl.
  3. Beat the egg white until it is frothy. Add sugar and a pinch of cream of tartar. Beat until a peak is formed.
  4. Fold in the almond flour mixture and a few drops of red/pink food coloring.
  5. Pipe small circles onto the baking sheet. Tap sheet to release air bubbles.
  6. Let it sit for 30-60 minutes until the skin is formed.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes at 300 degrees Fahrenheit
  8. Add the raspberry jam for filling.
  9. Add some fresh raspberries on top.
  10. Refrigerate for 24 hours.

Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients

  • 4 oz bittersweet chocolate, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 Fahrenheit (220 Celsius). Grease and flour four 6-ounce ramekins.
  2. In a bowl, melt chocolate and butter over a double boiler or microwave in 20 second, stirring until smooth.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, powdered sugar, and salt.
  4. In another bowl, beat eggs, egg yolks, and vanilla extract until light and fluffy.
  5. Gradually mix the melted chocolate into the egg mixture, then fold in the dry ingredients until just combined.
  6. Divide batter evenly into the prepared ramekins and place on a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 12-14 minutes, until the edges are firm, but the center is slightly soft.
  8. Let cool for 1-2 minutes, then run a knife around the edges and serve onto plates. Serve immediately.

No-Bake Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 16 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream, chilled

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar. Press firmly into a 9 inch pan.
  2. In a bowl, beat cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form, then gently fold into the cream cheese mixture.
  4. Spread the mixture evenly over the prepared crust.
  5. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight for best results.
  6. Before serving, top with fresh fruit, chocolate drizzle, or caramel sauce.

Classic Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 2 tablespoon coffee liqueur
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 8 oz mascarpone cheese, softened
  • 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream, chilled
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions

  1. Mix coffee and liqueur in a shallow dish and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, whisk egg yolks and sugar together, then place over a pot of simmering water, whisking until thickened. Remove from heat.
  3. Stir in mascarpone cheese until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form, then gently fold into the mascarpone mixture.
  5. Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture and arrange in a single layer in a baking dish.
  6. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers and repeat the layers.
  7. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  8. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.

Strawberry Shortcake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pound fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream, chilled
  • 1 tablespoon powdered sugar (for whipped cream)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 Fahrenheit (220 Celsius). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cut in cold butter using a pastry cutter or fork until the mixture resembles crumbs.
  4. Stir in milk and vanilla extract until combined and do not overmix.
  5. Drop dough onto the baking sheet in 6-8 rounds and bake for 12-15 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Toss sliced strawberries with sugar and let sit for 10 minutes.
  7. In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream and powdered sugar until soft peaks form.
  8. Slice shortcakes in half, layer with strawberries and whipped cream, and top with the other half.

Peanut Butter Cups

Ingredients

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 cups chocolate chips (milk or dark)
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Line a mini muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a bowl, mix peanut butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  3. Melt chocolate chips and butter together in a bowl over simmering water or in the microwave for 20 seconds, stirring frequently.
  4. Spoon a small amount of melted chocolate into each liner, then add a small dollop of the peanut butter mixture.
  5. Top with more melted chocolate and gently tap the tray to level the layers.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour until firm and serve.

New Orleans-Style Beignets

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 cup whole milk, warm
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • Cocoa powder for dusting
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • 1 cup powdered sugar, for dusting

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the warm milk and active dry yeast. Whisk until the yeast dissolves. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. Add the sugar, salt, eggs, butter, and vanilla extract to the yeast mixture. Stir until well combined.
  3. Mix in the flour, stirring continuously until a soft, slightly sticky dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough for 3-4 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Transfer the dough to a lightly greased bowl, cover with plastic wrap and a kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour.
  6. Roll the dough out into a large square about 1/2 inch thick. Using a pizza cutter, slice into 2.5 by 2.5 inch squares.
  7. In a fryer, heat vegetable oil to 350 Fahrenheit (175 Celsius).
  8. Carefully drop a few dough squares into the hot oil, making sure not to overcrowd the pot.
  9. Fry for 1-2 minutes per side, flipping once, until golden brown and serve.

Red Velvet Pizookies

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 1/2 sticks unsalted butter, melted (3/4 cup)
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 large egg + 1 egg yolk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 to 1/2 cup white chocolate chips
  • 1/3 cup semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon red food coloring

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, salt, and cornstarch.
  2. In a separate mixing bowl, combine the melted butter, white sugar, brown sugar, egg, egg yolk, and vanilla extract. Whisk until smooth.
  3. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until a soft dough forms.
  4. Fold in the white chocolate chips and semi-sweet chocolate chunks.
  5. Cover the dough and refrigerate for 30 minutes to enhance the texture and flavor.
  6. Preheat oven to 350 Fahrenheit (175 Celsius).
  7. Grease mini skillets or ramekins with butter or cooking spray to prevent sticking.
  8. Bake for 12-14 minutes, or until the edges are golden but the center remains slightly soft.
  9. Let the pizookies cool for a couple of minutes and serve.

Earl Grey Creme Brulee

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 2 Earl Grey tea bags
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 Fahrenheit.
  2. Heat the cream and Earl Grey tea bags in a pan until simmering and remove the tea bags.
  3. Slowly add warm cream while whisking the egg yolks and sugar.
  4. Add vanilla extract.
  5. Pour the mixture into a ceramic bowl and bake it in a water bath for 40-45 minutes.
  6. Chill the custards, sprinkle with sugar on top of the custard, and caramelize with a kitchen fire torch.

Biscoff Truffles

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Biscoff spread
  • 2 cups of powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Mix Biscoff spread, powdered sugar, graham cracker crumbs, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  2. Roll the mixture into small balls and place on a baking sheet.
  3. Freeze for 15 minutes.
  4. Dip each ball into melted chocolate and place back on the baking sheet.
  5. Refrigerate until the chocolate is set and serve.
